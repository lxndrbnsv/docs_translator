<h2> Registration of a personal account at Otkritie Bank </h2>
<p> You can access your personal account remotely without visiting an ATM or bank office if you have any valid Otkritie bank card. Online banking is registered on the official website at <strong> https://ib.open.ru/r </strong>. </p>
