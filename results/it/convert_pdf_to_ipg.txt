<h3> Portable Document Format </h3>
<p> PDF is an electronic document format developed by Adobe Systems using several features of the PostScript language. The official program for viewing documents in this format is Adobe Reader. Most often, a PDF file is a combination of text with bitmap and vector graphics, text forms, scripts written in JavaScript, and other elements. </p>
<h3> Joint Photography Experts Group </h3>
<p> JPG extension assigned to image files. Lots of photography and web graphics are saved in JPG. For compression purposes, bitmaps are saved in .jpg, making it easy to transfer and download these files over the Internet. The JPG format is based on a 24-bit color palette. The higher the level of compression used to create the JPG file, the worse the image quality becomes when it is unpacked. </p>
